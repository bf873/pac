The Story Behind the Lens

I started photographing live concerts over 10 years ago, armed with a borrowed camera and an obsession with capturing raw human emotion. What began in sweaty underground venues has evolved into capturing some of the industry's biggest moments.

Mission

To create images that don't just document a performance—they resurrect the feeling. Every frame is a time machine back to that electric moment on stage.

"Your music deserves photography that matches its energy. That's what I deliver."

With technical mastery and an artist's eye, I cut through the noise of thousands of frames to find THE shot—the one that captures who you are when the spotlight hits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my photos?

+

I offer same-day or next-day delivery for social media previews. Fully edited, organized galleries arrive within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery available upon request.

What's included in your packages?

+

Full event coverage, professional editing, high-res files, social-optimized versions, organized galleries, and basic retouching. Custom packages available for special needs.

Can you provide images for commercial use / press?

+

Yes. All deliverables include full commercial and press use rights. You own the images for all promotional and editorial applications.

Do you travel for events?

+

Absolutely. Travel is arranged based on event location. Travel costs are quoted separately or bundled into package rates depending on distance and scope.

What if the lighting is terrible?

+

I specialize in challenging lighting. Fast glass (f/2.8 or wider), high-ISO mastery, and careful white balance correction are core to my skill set. You'll still get stunning results.

How do I book you?

+

DM me on Instagram with your event details, date, and any specific requests. We'll discuss your needs, confirm availability, and finalize a custom quote.

Do you offer retouching or custom edits after delivery?

+

Yes. Minor revisions are included. More extensive edits are available à la carte. I want you to love every image—let's make it happen.
